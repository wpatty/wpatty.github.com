This is my portfolio website for HIST 697 Creating History in New Media. The purpose of the class is to learn about web design and how to use best practices for the creation of digital history. On the left, you will find links to the course assignments.

My research interests include mass transportation and urban history in the United States, particularly in the 20th century.
